Our Philosophy

Where Stillness Meets Support

Our classes blend the gentle support of aerial hammocks with the deep rest of restorative yoga and the clarity of guided meditation. Each session is designed to help you release tension, quiet the mind, and reconnect with your body.

Whether you are new to yoga or a seasoned practitioner, our approach meets you exactly where you are. No flexibility required, no experience necessary — just a willingness to slow down and breathe.

Meet Your Teacher

A Journey Rooted in Rest & Renewal

With over a decade of practice and teaching, I discovered that the most transformative moments in yoga happen not in striving, but in surrendering. That insight led me to specialise in aerial restorative yoga and meditation.

After training in Bali, India, and across Europe, I developed a unique approach that combines the decompressive benefits of aerial hammocks with the deep stillness of restorative postures and mindful meditation.

My mission is simple: to create a space where you feel safe enough to truly let go. Every class is an invitation to rest, restore, and remember what it feels like to be at peace.

What We Offer

Our Offerings

Aerial Restorative Yoga

Experience the unique sensation of being cradled in a silk hammock while your body releases tension through supported, gravity-assisted postures. Perfect for decompressing the spine, opening the hips, and calming the nervous system.

  • Spinal decompression
  • Deep hip opening
  • Nervous system regulation
  • Full body support

Restorative Yoga

Settle into deeply supported floor-based postures using bolsters, blankets, and blocks. Each pose is held for extended periods, allowing your body to soften and your mind to quieten. Includes guided meditation to close each session.

  • Extended supported holds
  • Prop-supported comfort
  • Guided meditation
  • Stress release

Private Sessions & Workshops

Tailored one-on-one sessions or small group workshops designed around your specific needs. Whether you are recovering from injury, managing stress, or deepening your practice, these sessions offer personalised attention and care.

  • Personalised approach
  • Injury-sensitive options
  • Small group workshops
  • Corporate wellness
